Hi! We are Erin and Carrie, and we are so glad you have visited our shop! The two of us met in 2011 at our "day jobs" and immediately bonded over our love of shopping and our passion for fashion! We have spent a lot of time and money shopping in specialty plus-sized clothing stores. We loved the quality of their clothing but felt that these stores take advantage of the limited plus-sized retail options, and really gouge you on the prices. Sassy and Sweet Clothing Boutique was born when we realized that there was a better way! We decided to open our own shop where we could offer quality plus-sized clothing at affordable prices. Every item is hand-picked personally by us, thoughtfully chosen to fit your curves and make you feel beautiful! We have high standards for what we will wear, and we vow to never sell something in our store that we wouldn't wear ourselves. In fact, we model all of our items on our site to allow you to see how they look on "real" people (not just models), and on different body types. Our goal is to help you find your perfect item on the first try, so we offer sizing and fit input to help you choose the correct size. Happy shopping!
